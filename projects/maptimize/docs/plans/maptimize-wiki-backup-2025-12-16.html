Process Steps

  1. TM finds a suitable Service Review date for the customer and sends out invites for the Service Review and Service Review Prep to all involved.
  2. TM creates a ticket for MSPE/EO and DO using the wiki form.
  3. MSPE/EO Jira gets populated by automation with data and draft PPT, DO uses tools to extract required data.
  4. MSPE, EO, DO review/analyse further their respective parts and document their findings. MSPE in JIRA, DO and EO directly in the draft PPT.
  5. TM, MSPE, EO attend a prep call to discuss findings, changes and follow up actions from previous SR and aob.
  6. TM finalizes the PPT with MSPE analysis, extra slides from TMs template and any additional details coming from the prep call, saves the PPT in sharepoint and associated JIRA.
  7. Service Review call held with the customer, TM , MSPE and EO
  8. TM sends post SR email with PDF slides, summary of the call and action points. TM opens an Advisory Request to MSPE/EO/DO if required
